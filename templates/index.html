About QUIZDOM

QUIZDOM is an advanced quiz platform that transforms learning into an engaging competitive experience. Built with cutting-edge web technologies, it offers a seamless interface for knowledge testing across multiple domains. Our intelligent scoring system adapts to your performance, while real-time leaderboards fuel healthy competition among quiz enthusiasts worldwide.

Features

  • 🧠 10+ Knowledge Categories: Science, History, Technology, Arts, Sports, Geography
  • ⚡ Three Difficulty Levels: Beginner (5pts), Intermediate (10pts), Expert (15pts)
  • 📊 Advanced Analytics: Real-time performance tracking and progress insights
  • ⏱️ Adaptive Timer System: Configurable time limits for enhanced challenge
  • 🏆 Global Leaderboards: Compete with quiz masters from around the world
  • 🎨 Modern UI/UX: Glassmorphism design with smooth animations
  • 📱 Fully Responsive: Perfect experience across all devices
  • 🔧 Customizable Settings: Personalize your quiz experience
